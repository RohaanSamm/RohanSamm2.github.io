Road Accident Dashboard (Excel)

✤ Created an interactive Road Accident Analytics dashboard in Excel, analyzing 2021-2022 road accident data for insights into casualties and accident conditions. Key metrics include total casualties post-accident (417,883), casualty distribution by accident severity and vehicle type (79.8% by car, minimal by others), monthly casualty trends, peak casualty months (November 2021 & 2022), and casualty distribution by road type (highest on single carriageways) and road surface (highest on dry surfaces). My approach included data cleaning, processing, analysis, and visualization, resulting in an interactive dashboard with slicers and timelines for seamless user experience and deeper insights into accident patterns.
